What is this?

The list is specifically curated to show the best freebies for each category, and should cover just about everything you could want.

Words of Wisdom

And some FAQ

Is all of this free?

Yes. Everything on this page is free to use both personally and commercially, no attribution required and no strings attached.

Pretty GUIs

Don't let a pretty GUI get to your head. There are plenty of ugly plugins that sound amazing, as well as eye candy ones that don't.

More more MORE!!

Just because you downloaded another plugin, your tracks won't automatically become better. An artist needs mediums and paints to do their work with, but at the end of the day, more paintbrushes won't make a better painter.

How do I use X?

Read the manual! Seriously, disregarding the complete step-by-step guide that the developer put together doesn't make you cool. If there's no manual available, then Google is your friend.

Nice, I have paintbrushes. How do I paint?

With the internet, there are now thousands of different resources on how to learn music for free. Some are linked on the footer of this page, and others you'll have to do searches for yourself. It's incredibly easy to find tutorials. Experiment and try new things!

No one's listening to my music

Well first, you have to be skilled enough to make music that people enjoy. This will come to everyone with time and practice. However, there are various marketing strategies (again, Google) that can help your music reach a wider audience.
